Come, Stay, Play!

Walking

Fun-filled one-hour walk with up to 4 dogs in total. We have a 1:2 walker to dog rule to ensure safety. Collection & drop off (after a rub-down!) included. After every walk you will receive photos & updates via our bespoke app!

Day-Care

Drop-off & collect from our specialised day-care centre. 1-hour walk included. Your pooch can play & socialise, or simply hang out in our dedicated chill-out area!

Boarding

Going away on holiday? Our qualified staff will stay overnight with your woofer & provide constant companionship. We will send you twice-daily updates via our app to keep you in-the-loop.

About Us

We are a team of floofydoof-fanatics, offering dog-sitting services in and around Exmoor National Park.
You might see us sometimes out and about in our floofy-transport van...
we’re usually on our way to or from a walk with our merry gang of doggos!
When we’re not rambling across the hills of Exmoor or playing fetch on the beach,
we’re back at our bespoke doggy-day-care centre hanging with the pack.
