Who We Are...

Floofy Doof Dog Care is a team of three dog-crazy-hoomans offering floof-sitting services in and around Exmoor National Park.

You might see us sometimes when we're out-and-about in our Floofy Doof transport-van. We’re usually on our way to or from a walk with our merry gang of doggos!

When we’re not rambling across the hills of Exmoor or playing fetch on the beach, we’re back at our bespoke doggy-day-care-facility, hanging with the pack!

We'd love to meet you...why not pay us a visit?
